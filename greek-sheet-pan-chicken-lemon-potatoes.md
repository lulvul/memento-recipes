# **Greek Sheet-Pan Chicken & Lemon Potatoes**

**Serves 8 · 60 min**

## **Ingredients**

### Chicken and potatoes
- 8 chicken thighs
- 1200 g potatoes, cut in wedges
- 90 g olive oil
- 2 lemons, juiced
- 8 g dried oregano
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- salt, to taste
- black pepper, to taste

### Tzatziki
- 300 g greek yogurt
- 1 cucumber, grated
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 14 g olive oil
- salt, to taste

## **Steps**

1. Heat the oven to 425°F (220°C).

2. Toss potato wedges with half the olive oil, half the lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper. Spread on a sheet pan.

3. Rub chicken thighs with the remaining oil, lemon, garlic, salt, and pepper. Nestle them skin-up among the potatoes.

4. Roast 40-45 minutes until the chicken hits 175°F and the potatoes are golden, flipping the potatoes once halfway.

5. Make the tzatziki: squeeze the water out of the grated cucumber, then stir it into the yogurt with garlic, olive oil, and salt. Chill until serving.

6. Serve chicken and potatoes with tzatziki on the side. The toddler gets plain chicken and potato.
