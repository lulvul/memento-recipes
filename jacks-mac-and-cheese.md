# Mac and Cheese

## Ingredients
- 1 lb elbow pasta
- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp flour
- 3 cups whole milk
- 2 cups sharp cheddar, grated
- 1 cup gruyere, grated
- salt, pepper, pinch of nutmeg

## Steps

1. Boil pasta until just shy of al dente. Drain.

2. Make a roux: melt butter, whisk in flour, cook 1 minute.

3. Add milk slowly, whisking, until it thickens.

4. Kill the heat, melt in the cheese a handful at a time.

5. Combine with pasta, season, bake 20 min at 375.
