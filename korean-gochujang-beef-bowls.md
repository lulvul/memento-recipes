# **Korean Gochujang Beef Bowls**

**Serves 8 · 35 min**

## **Ingredients**

- 900 g ground beef
- 370 g jasmine rice
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 30 g ginger, grated
- 60 g gochujang
- 60 g soy sauce
- 28 g toasted sesame oil
- 24 g brown sugar
- 4 scallions, sliced
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 12 g sesame seeds
- salt, to taste

## **Steps**

1. Cook the jasmine rice.

2. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over high heat, breaking it up. Drain off excess fat.

3. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute until fragrant.

4. Stir in gochujang, soy sauce, brown sugar, and sesame oil. Simmer 3-4 minutes until glossy and the beef is fully coated.

5. Toss in half the scallions. Taste and salt.

6. Serve over rice, topped with the rest of the scallions, sesame seeds, and cucumber on the side. For the toddler, plate beef and rice before adding any extra gochujang heat.
