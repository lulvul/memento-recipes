MOROCCAN CHICKEN TAGINE WITH CHICKPEAS & APRICOTS

Serves 8 · 70 min


INGREDIENTS

- 8 chicken thighs
- 28 g olive oil
- 2 onions, diced
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 12 g ground cumin
- 6 g ground cinnamon
- 6 g ground ginger
- 8 g paprika
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- 480 g chicken stock
- 2 cans chickpeas, drained
- 150 g dried apricots, halved
- 370 g couscous
- salt, to taste
- black pepper, to taste


STEPS

1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper. Brown it in the olive oil in a large pot, then set aside.

2. Soften the onions in the same pot, about 5 minutes. Add garlic, cumin, cinnamon, ginger, and paprika; bloom 1 minute.

3. Return the chicken. Add tomatoes, stock, chickpeas, and apricots. Simmer covered 35-40 minutes until the chicken is tender.

4. Uncover and simmer 10 minutes to thicken. Salt to taste.

5. Cook the couscous: pour an equal weight of boiling stock or water over it, cover 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.

6. Serve the tagine over couscous. Even better reheated the next day. It is sweet, not spicy, so the toddler can eat it as-is.
