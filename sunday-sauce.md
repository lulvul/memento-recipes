# **Sunday Sauce**

**Serves 6 · 3h 30m**

## **Ingredients**

- 28 g olive oil
- 450 g pork ribs or italian sausage
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, sliced
- 2 cans san marzano tomatoes, crushed by hand
- 240 g red wine
- basil, salt, pepper, to taste

## **Steps**

1. Brown the meat in oil, then set aside.

2. Soften onion and garlic in the same pot.

3. Deglaze with the wine, scrape the bottom.

4. Add tomatoes, return the meat, drop in basil.

5. Simmer low, lid cracked, 3 hours. Stir now and then.
