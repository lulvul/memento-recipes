WEEKNIGHT CHILI

Serves 4 · 50 min


INGREDIENTS

- 450 g ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 16 g chili powder
- 6 g ground cumin
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- 1 can kidney beans, drained
- 240 g beef stock


STEPS

1. Brown the beef in a heavy pot. Push to the side.

2. Soften onion and garlic in the fat.

3. Stir in chili powder and cumin, bloom 30 seconds.

4. Add tomatoes, beans, and stock. Simmer 30-40 min.

5. Salt to taste. Better the next day.
