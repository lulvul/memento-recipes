# Weeknight Chili

<!-- meta: serves=4 | time=50m | tags=chili,weeknight,one-pot -->

## Ingredients
- 450 g ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 16 g chili powder
- 6 g ground cumin
- 400 g crushed tomatoes
- 240 g kidney beans, drained
- 240 g beef stock

## Steps

1. Brown the beef in a heavy pot. Push to the side.
2. Soften onion and garlic in the fat.
3. Stir in chili powder and cumin, bloom 30 seconds.
4. Add tomatoes, beans, and stock. Simmer 30-40 min.
5. Salt to taste. Better the next day.
